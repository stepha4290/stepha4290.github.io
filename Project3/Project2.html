HTML and CSS Project: Sales Website



Project 2 consists of creating a sales webpage. The purpose of project 2 is to apply the knowledge of colors,image manipulation, animation, slideshow, and modal window using CSS to create a simple sales page.

I create a website about Josephina Body Care Collection. On this page I presented my favorite body care products. In the introuduction, I created a slideshow to advertise the product sales, and I used hero images to present the body care products. In the prodcts list, I presented three lotions and three sets of my favorite body care. I also designed a modal window to present each product.
